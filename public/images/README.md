Please place your logo image file here as **`logo.png`**

## Logo Specifications

The logo should be:
- Format: PNG (recommended) or SVG
- Size: 500x500px or larger (will be automatically resized)
- Background: Transparent or white
- Design: Circular emblem with:
  - "MERCADO" at the top
  - "LA RIQUEZA" on the green banner
  - "BY MONICA HAQUE, INC." at the bottom
